Don't See The Invitation You're Looking For?
If you received an invitation to bid from the BidPlanroom system, but do not see it in your invitation list...
Here may be the reasons why:
  • The original invitation email was not sent to you (example: someone forwarded you the invitation)
  • You did not log in or create your account with the same email address the invitation was sent to. There is no need to create another account. You can change your account email address here
  • If you do not want to change your account email, or want to have an invitation sent directly to your account email, please contact the General Contractor that originally sent the invitation and they can assist you. The General Contractor's contact information can be located in the invitation email.
If the project is listed publicly, you can also try to find the project in our Project Search. If you have any other issues or questions you can contact BidPlanroom Support for assistance.
